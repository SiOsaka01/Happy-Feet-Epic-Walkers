About Us

We are a walking group composed of men from Oldham and surrounding districts whom are linked to Andys Man Club. We are all going through a storm of some kind and together we will weather our difficulties together. This walking group is oriented towards increasing our personal wellbeing by easing ourselves back into exercise and talking to one another whilst covering the paths of our walks. We are a closed group, members are restricted to AMC Oldham and friends.
